Learning Management System

Asia-Pacific (APAC) is expected to witness the significant growth rate during the forecast period owing to demand for cost effective and improved learning experience (Markets & Markets, Web Report).

APAC organizations are increasingly opting for Learning Management System (LMS) to gain better returns on investments made on talent development, employee learning requirements, proper employee training, and receiving quantifiable business outcomes. As many organizations have tight learning budgets, they prefer a technology-enabled learning approach, which is also a cost effective way to offer training and resources to their learners. DuoTech Global makes your Learning Management System (LMS) also known as e-Learning Portal, easier by integrating the necessary hardware and software which gives you a complete usable platform. This LMS is based on Modular Object-Oriented Dynamic Learning Environment which is open source and customized for your requirement. It is an e-learning tool that can offer simple and safe solutions to any institution, no matter how large or small, be it an individual teacher or a huge university. LMS facilitates online collaborations, which can be used for teacher-to-student, teacher-to-teacher or student-to-student.
